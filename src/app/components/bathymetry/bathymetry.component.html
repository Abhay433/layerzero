1. Live Acoustic Data Ingestion

LyzoAI streams raw acoustic data directly from onboard multibeam and echosounder systems.

  • Supports EK60, EK80
  • Reson 7125, T20, T51
  • EM2040 / 2040c / 2040p (including Dual TX / Dual RX)
  • EM710, EM3002, EM302, EM122
  • ME70 Bathymetry Module
  • Time-synchronized input with navigation, attitude, and sound velocity data
  • Processing occurs in real time, with no interruption to acquisition

2. Intelligent Preprocessing & Signal Conditioning

Incoming depth and intensity measurements are automatically standardised and conditioned.

  • Beam-wise corrections for motion, refraction, and system geometry
  • Adaptive filtering of water-column noise and acoustic artefacts
  • Dynamic detection of dropouts, spikes, and multipath returns
  • System-aware handling of dual TX / dual RX configurations

3. AI-Based Cleaning & Adaptive Quality Control

LyzoAI applies self-learning models that evolve with survey conditions.

  • Automated outlier rejection for bathymetry and backscatter
  • Context-aware thresholds adapting to depth, seabed roughness, and vessel speed
  • Continuous confidence scoring for each sounding and intensity measurement
  • Real-time visual feedback on data quality and coverage gaps

4. On-the-Fly Bathymetric Gridding

Cleaned depth soundings are gridded immediately during acquisition.

  • Resolution adapts dynamically to depth and footprint
  • Seamless surface updates as new data arrives
  • Detection of artefacts such as line offsets, striping, or beam-edge noise
  • Instant assessment of coverage completeness and overlap

5. Real-Time Backscatter Processing & Normalisation

Backscatter intensity is processed in parallel with bathymetry.

  • Angle, range, and transmission-loss normalisation
  • Correction for system-specific and survey-dependent effects
  • Generation of real-time backscatter mosaics
  • Preservation of relative intensity for sediment and texture analysis

6. Seabed Insight & Preliminary Classification

Bathymetry and backscatter are analysed jointly to provide immediate seabed context.

  • Identification of textural contrasts and seabed boundaries
  • Highlighting of anomalous intensity zones
  • Optional clustering of backscatter responses for preliminary substrate discrimination
  • Supports rapid decision-making while still offshore

7. Delivery & Survey Feedback

LyzoAI delivers decision-ready products before the survey ends.

  • Export of clean bathymetric grids and backscatter mosaics
  • Continuous QC layers and confidence maps
  • Reduced dependency on heavy post-processing
  • Outputs suitable for hydrography, engineering, habitat mapping, and geoscience workflows
