Universal Data Ingestion

  • Seamless integration with Reson 7125, T20, T51, EM2040, EM710, EM3002, EM302, EM122, and EK60, EK80, ME70 systems.
  • Direct ingestion of raw acoustic data without interrupting active acquisition software.
  • Time-synchronized input of navigation, attitude, and sound velocity for high-precision results.

Signal Conditioning

  • Automatic beam-wise corrections for vessel motion and water-column refraction.
  • Real-time removal of water-column noise and acoustic artifacts via adaptive filtering.
  • Dynamic identification of dropouts, spikes, and multipath returns during the survey.

AI Cleaning & QC

  • Self-learning models provide automated outlier rejection for bathymetry and backscatter.
  • Context-aware thresholds that adapt to depth, seabed roughness, and vessel speed.
  • Continuous reliability scoring for every sounding and intensity measurement.

Real-Time Gridding

  • Grid resolution adapts automatically based on depth and footprint size.
  • Seamlessly updates the bathymetric surface as new data packets arrive.
  • Instant detection of line offsets, striping, or beam-edge noise while on station.

Backscatter & Insights

  • Normalization of angle, range, and transmission loss to provide true seabed intensity.
  • Joint analysis of bathymetry and backscatter for seabed boundary identification.
  • Optional clustering for preliminary seabed classification and substrate discrimination.

Delivery & Feedback

  • Export clean bathymetric grids and backscatter mosaics before the vessel returns to port.
  • Drastically minimizes dependency on heavy manual post-processing tasks.
  • Standard outputs optimized for hydrography, offshore engineering, and habitat mapping.
